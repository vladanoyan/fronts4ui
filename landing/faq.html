S4FE is a decentralised global network which enables it's users to:
- register personal property / items on the blockchain
- report personal items as lost or stolen
- search for items
- verify items, in order to identify if they are reported as lost or stolen
- report the discovery of lost and stolen items to the network
- enable the return of a discovered item and receive rewards
Our goal ist to enable the discovery and retrieval of lost and stolen items and to reduce crime, by increasing its risk.
