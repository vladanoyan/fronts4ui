Who we are

S4FE is the world’s first blockchain-based global database for the registration and identification of stolen and lost items. The database is accessible to everyone and enables users to register and search for property. The types of property include electronic devices, jewelry, personal collections, valuables of any kind, documents, sports and hobby equipment, vehicles and machines and any other conceivable item representing value to its owner.

Solutions

S4FE is an online application and security organisation creating easily accessible solutions to reduce and prevent the loss or theft of valuables whilst at the same time enabling identification and retrieval of such items.

To accomplish this, S4FE is operating multiple layers of software, hardware and personnel to establish tangible ways to register, identify and return uniquely identifiable items:

Decentralized transparent cryptographic registration and query network for items

Bounty incentivized recovery and return of lost & stolen items.

Tracking and securing of special interest items.

Open API to enable service providers and law enforcement.

S4FE - Serial

Users register assets through various features of the interface. Private users can register an unlimited amount of assets per account. Depending on volume and individual requirements, corporate accounts may be subject to special rates and plans which are still to be determined.

Asset registration requires unique identifcation vectors, such as serial numbers, in combination with an assignment to matching product categories and asset speci cations.

As an alternative, i.e. when no serial number is available, visual features can be collected via photo and / or video to enable pattern recognition. Additional item description should always be made by text or photo and / or video for all registered assets. As soon as an asset has been registered in the network, users can instantly report a theft or loss through website, app or call center. Once an asset is reported as lost or stolen it will immediately (real-time) be tagged as such in the network and recognizable as lost or stolen item to users worldwide.

Pattern Recognition

Pattern recognition is the automated recognition of patterns and regularities in data. Pattern recognition is closely related to artificial intelligence and machine learning, together with applications such as data mining and knowledge discovery in databases (KDD), and is often used interchangeably with these terms.

What does S4FE use pattern recognition for?

Not everything has a unique serial number like a watch or a laptop.

For example an owner of a pet might upload an image of his dog via pattern recognition to the blockchain. If the dog runs away, another S4FE platform user can identify the dog’s owner via our application

The Token Sale

The token sale will offer 300.000.000 tokens to the public.

Tokens will be sold using a smart contract until all tokens have been sold.

The price will be set in Ether (“ETH”) before the sale and buyers will receive ERC–20 tokens at the conclusion of the sale.

The sale will take place in different stages — measured in blocks on the Ethereum blockchain — or until all 300 million tokens are sold.

Tokens will be delivered to token buyers via a smart contract and transferred after the Initial Coin Offering.

The soft-cap for the sale is $USD5 million the equivalent in Ether will be set on the start date of the sale.

The target hard cap is $USD50 million at the time of the sale in Ether.
